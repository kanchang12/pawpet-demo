How it started

It started with one dog called Biscuit.

When one of our founders had a family emergency, the kennels were full and the sitters they found online had no reviews, no checks, and no easy way to book. Turns out every neighbour had the same story.

What began as a shared spreadsheet of trusted sitters has grown into a proper local network — around {{ sitter_count }} vetted sitters and {{ customer_count }}+ regular customers across Yorkshire and the wider North.
